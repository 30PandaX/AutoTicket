AutoTicket

To Copy:

  1. In Sprinklr, double-click on a message to select it. Most of the screen will darken, highlighting a pane on the right.
  2. Press Alt+C to copy.

To Paste:

  1. In Elementool, create a New Issue.
  2. Press Alt+V to fill in the ticket.
